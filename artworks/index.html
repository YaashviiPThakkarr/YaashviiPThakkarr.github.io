Artworks

I am highly passionate about art and designing since I was a kid. I used to go to a fine arts school over weekends during my schools days. When I was young, I used to make paintings using water color, oil paints, canvas and I loved to experiment with various textures. (as you can see in my paintings below) Now, I have picked up doodling on Procreate and have my Instagram channel as well.

Check out My doodles on Instagram
Here are some of my artworks and I hope you have fun going through them!

Abstract Painting with Still Life(Old Earthen Pot)

I love playing around with texture and this was made using
a plactic film and skecthed the pot using charcoal!
This painting was selected in National Exhibitions in Gujarat, India (2016).

Basil's Cathedral, Moscow

I have always wanted to see this cathedral that was
the inspiration behind this painting.
I used oil and water along with water colors
for the background texture.

Wall Painting

I made this painting by reminiscing my days while
painting walls in varioud competitions that I participated in
with my friends. We made countless memory
and it was super fun! I miss those days.
I have used salt with pastels to get
the rough wall like texture.

Adventurous Amusement Parkr

I love thrilling amusement parks and I made this painting
after I visited Six Flags, New England in Boston, Massachusets.
I fell in love with a lot many roller coasters but Bizzaro
was the inspiration of this paiting. /span>

Wedding day

This is a dreamy India bride, a woman who adorns so
many roles but on her wedding day - she's starts her journey
with a bigger family. This is so symbolize
the dreams of the bride and the her dreamy thoughts
as depicted by the clouds in the background.

Disney in my fav colors

Who doesn't love DisneyLand? And I love
the color blue, so I painted my favorite place as a kid in
blue with jute bag's texture in the background.

Tree of hope

This painting's main highlight is that it has a
tree of hope. Hope is symbolized by all the colors used in the
bakcground - depicting human emotions and
ups and downs in a person's life.
But despite all the ups and downs, there's hope
just like the tree which doesn't stops to grow,
taking each experience as a learning opportunity.

Warli Painting

This is an Indian house in a village which is deeply rooted
to the historic culture, represented by the warli paintings on
the walls. Warli painting is a
tribal art style.

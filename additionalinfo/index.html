    Some fun facts about me! ^-^

  • I used to go to a fine arts school every weekend throughout my school days.
    Those were the best weekends I have ever had.
    Each painting required a gazillion strokes and shade mixing,
    which eventually increased my perseverance and tolerance.
  • I have infinite affection for the color blue. Any shade of
    blue can make my day. I jokingly say, in case we ever
    bicker, wear blue and we’re good to go.
  • Photos are my life. I love capturing moments and
    getting clicked. If there’s anyone who an use 128GB
    a year, that’s me! My fascination helped me learn photography and
    explore the editing tools.
  • I’m a trained tennis player, and I have represented
    my city in the district level. Late night tennis matches are
    a routine in my family and I am highly competitive about it.
  • My grand mom has taught card games to
    all the seven kids in my family (including me) and we often have
    mini card game tournaments at home. I realized how cards have improved
    my decision making skills and confidence level.
  • I had never imagined I would be able to stay without internet
    for a day, but nature and its beauty flipped the coin. I went on a
    cruising trip to Alaska with my family where I happily survived
    without internet. Norwegian Bliss was indeed surreal and
    the blue ocean with icebergs was mesmerizing.
